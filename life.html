STARTING WITH FRESHERS

The new faces of the computer department are welcomed by an amazing freshers party. Thers's introduction, tasks that are to be performed by first year students to be MR.Fresher or Miss.Fresher.Dance performances, singing and poetry performed by all the students. Sophomores are given this responsibility to organise this event. Feast is also arranged for all the students who are a part of department. And then for a blast there's DJ......Nothing more to elaborate then.

Techfest

"Cynosure" an event conducted throughout the University is focussed on the concept "ENGGOY"=" Engineering +Joy ".There are several competitions arranged by the students of the university of all the branches. There are cash prizes too. Sponsorsips are arranged by the students for this type of events. Overall, this builds every skill you require to get success. It's Fun......!!!!!!

There is also Cultural fest just after the Techfest. This college festival focusses on the cultural events like singing, poetry, dances, drama, skits, and much more..

Heard of Days at College??..

Eversince a student comes to know about "#Days" he/she wants to experience it. Not only first year students but also all seniors are also very excited for these days. A final year student may be excited to create some last memories in the college. A third year student may want to propose his/her crush. A sophomore may be excited just to click some wild photos with his/her freind. And a first year student may wanna experience college life. For every person these #days means something, it has some importance. Must experience$$$...
